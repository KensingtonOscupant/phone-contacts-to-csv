Here's how you can do that on both Android and iOS devices:</p><h3>Android</h3><ol><li>Open the "Contacts" app on your Android device.</li><li>Tap the three vertical dots in the top right corner of the screen to open the menu.</li><li>Select "Export" from the menu.</li><li>Choose where you want to save the exported file, such as your Google Drive or Dropbox account.</li><li>A VCF file containing screen.</li><li>Select "All Contacts" from the list.</li><li>Tap the "Select" button in the top right corner of the screen.</li><li>Tap on each contact you wish to export or tap "Select All" in the top left corner of the screen.</li><li>Tap the "Share" button in the bottom left corner of the screen.</li><li>Select "Mail" or "AirDrop" to send the exported contacts as a VCF file to your email or
